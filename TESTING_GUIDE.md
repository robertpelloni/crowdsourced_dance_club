# User Testing Guide: CrowdClub Experience Refinement

## Introduction
Thank you for participating in the CrowdClub User Testing phase. Your feedback is critical to refining our algorithmic Conductor and ensuring a seamless, high-energy dancefloor experience.

## Testing Objectives
1. **Validate Vibe Accuracy:** Does the "Match %" feel musically correct?
2. **Assess Transition Quality:** Are the crossfades and HPF sweeps smooth?
3. **Verify System Stability:** Does the real-time sync hold up under usage?
4. **Evaluate Gamification:** Do Vibe Points and Streaks feel rewarding?

## Testing Scenarios

### Scenario 1: The Newcomer
- **Action:** Register a new account and browse the catalog.
- **KPI:** Is the onboarding/sync process intuitive?

### Scenario 2: The Influencer
- **Action:** Request a high-match song (>80%) and get a "Vibe Streak."
- **KPI:** Does the gamification feedback (badges/notifications) trigger correctly?

### Scenario 3: The Critic
- **Action:** Request an incompatible song (e.g., Ambient into Psytrance).
- **KPI:** Is the "Request Denied" feedback clear and musically justified?

### Scenario 4: The Crowd
- **Action:** Coordinate with others to vote a specific track to the top of the queue.
- **KPI:** Does the queue reorder in real-time across all devices?

## Reporting Feedback
Use the **"Refine"** tab in the Web Prototype or Mobile App to submit your ratings:
- **Vibe Accuracy:** 1-5 stars.
- **Technical Smoothness:** 1-5 stars.
- **Comments:** Be specific about any audio glitches or UI friction.

## Technical Glitch Reporting
If you encounter a "critical buzzkill" (app crash or audio drop), please report:
1. Your Device/Browser.
2. The exact timestamp of the glitch.
3. The track that was playing or transitioning.
